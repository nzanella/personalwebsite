Sworn translations


  • Private or business deeds,
  • Certificates
  • Licences or contracts
Once a translation is done, Finking can also swear it before the justice of the peace – this can be done for private or business deeds, for certificates and licences or contracts and legal documents. Here the translator swears that she/he has done the translation with utmost care and as faithfully as possible.

An additional service is the authentication of the signature of the civil servant that has signed a document and is performed at the Public Prosecutor’s Office or the Prefecture.
